Our Flagship Events

ISC 2021

The International Student Conference 2021 brought together some top academia to present and share research details. It furnished a wonderful opportunity for members as well as non-members to build networking, receive feedback and learn about emerging technologies that have the potential to influence future developments in biomedical engineering and healthcare technology.

This exclusive conference held for a time span of 3 days had the theme of “Advances in Biomedical Sciences, AI and Industrial Revolution 4.0 for a Cost-Effective and Sustainable Future”. Along with eminent keynotes, the conference also featured numerous mini-symposia, special sessions, workshops, research presentations, Industrial conclave, academic sessions for students and young professionals, and a virtual exhibition hall.

Event Closed

Human Forensics

One of the key highlights of Gravitas remains to be "Human Forensics" year after year. It receives a multitude of participants from diverse backgrounds. This one-day event is the perfect crossover of education and thrill. It involves the demonstrations of awesome forensic technologies that are used to sneak into evidence to catch what does not meet the eye. In other words, it is just the right place to unearth the Sherlock in you!

Event Closed

CWC - Coffee With CEO

A series of live interviews, where we brew and enjoy a cup of coffee with some of the leading entrepreneurs, lures a large audience.

These "Coffee with CEO" sessions introduce the student community to vivid aspects of making important company decisions, building business strategies, and shaping the organizational culture. Our eminent guests share their life experiences which become a treasure trove for young participants to pave their way to success.
